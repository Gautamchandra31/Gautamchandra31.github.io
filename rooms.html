    FAQs

    Q1) What is the best time to visit Kalipokhri and Sandakphu?

    Oct to Dec will be the best season because probably the sky will be more clear and people will get a view of all the peaks and there is less chance of rainfall. In the month of Dec to Jan, you will get to experience snowfall. This route is all time trek and every season has a different view.

    Q2) When your homestay is open?

    Yalung Homestay is open 24*7 all season you have to leave your car in Kalipokhri and walk from there to Sandakphu. It will be 6KM... Advanced bookings in Sandakphu are mandatory in the peak season or else you can bring your own tent.

    Q3) How to reach your homestay?

    The locals re-purposed these multi-terrain vehicles as commercial local transport between Sandakphu and Manebhanjan. The roads are almost non-existing, as it is mainly a trekking route. Only 1960s make old Land Rovers mostly driven in Kerosin oil.

    Q4) Is guide mandatory?

    Before you start the trek for Sandakphu, you need to ensure that you have a registered guide or a porter/ guide (which is now mandatory). If you are going through a Tour Operator, then they will arrange for the guide/porter.

    Q5) Which mobile networks are available there?

    Only NTC network is available for Nepalese and no network in Indian sim but wifi is available for voice call over Whatsapp.

    Q6) What are the modes of payment you accept?

    We accept both cash and UPI payments are also available for Indian visiter via Gpay in following number +91 88209 35532 (Tenzing Yongden Bhutia).
