Yalung Homestay

Enter as guests, and leave as friends.

This lovely homestay is located in Kalpokhri, Nepal. The untouched beauty of lush green Mountains, surreal sunset view, and the vibrant colors of nature will inspire you to stay here longer. on a clear day you get a majestic view of Himalayan peaks like sleeping buddha, Kanchenjunga, etc. There are many hidden nature trails that you can explore during your stay here.

A Home for Trekkers

People travelling to Sandakphu stay here, Sandakphu trekkers get pleasent stay in insulated rooms, personalized food is also provided. Blankets are washed after every stay and everything is kept very clean for sanitory purpose.

We are known for our hospitality, food, ambiance & location. We provide the best of our services and a conducive environment for our esteemed guests so that guests can enjoy the memorable time spent at our little villa. We provide high-quality accommodation along with breakfast/lunch/dinner for Indian as well as foreign tourists.

Kalipokhari Lake

Kalipokhri is place where water in the lake is dark. 'Kali' means 'dark' and 'pokhri' means 'lake' in Nepali

Sandakphu Hill

Sandakphu is the highest point of the Singalila Ridge in Darjeeling district on the West Bengal-Nepal border.

Distance: 6100m | Height: 3636m

Kalipokhri

Kalipokhri is a small Himalayan village inside the Singalila National Park in the Darjeeling subdivision.

Meghma Monastery

It is one of the prominent monasteries of the region because there are 108 Buddhas are believed to be encountered in ones journey from this world to the next world

Singalila National Park

Singalila National Park is a national park of India at an altitude of more than 7000 feet above sea level. It is well known for the trekking route to Sandakphu.
